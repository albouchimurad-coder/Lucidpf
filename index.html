Our Story

Built by film chemists.
Trusted by installers.

LUCID was founded fifteen years ago on a single premise: protection film should be engineered, not just coated. From our first roll off the line to today's global shipments, every batch is formulated, cast, and cured in our own American facility not outsourced, not relabeled.

We manufacture for the people who install our film every day. That means tighter tolerances, faster turnaround for distributors, and a supply chain we control end to end.
